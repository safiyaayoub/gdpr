General Data Protection Regulation

An important part of the EU GDPR (Law) is the timely fulfillment of the rights of the individuals affected. With our module, the data protection inquiries can be processed easily and efficiently. The four available operations cover all six of these rights.

Rights covered in the module:

  • Right of Access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object

Wizard

There is the option of four rights in the wizard. Each operation is explained in a short description upon selection. Subsequently, the data set (lead/opportunity, partner, user, applicant or employee), the name of the inquiring Partner and notes can be added.

Log

A log provides a complete overview of the requests/operations. Odoo Standard Encoding UTF-8 is used.

All personal information, which can be recorded in the Odoo standard, is covered by this module. In addition to the module, we also offer workshops and a roadmap to make sure all your processes are compliant - even beyond Odoo. If you have any further questions, do not hesitate to contact us.
